Punch

PUNCH is a quick, action based, top-down, chaotic party couch game that you can play with 2 to 4 players!

Be the last man standing by crushing, drowning or obliterating your enemy using various pickups and containers! Be careful though, containers keep falling from their place inside the dock and can crush you!

This game was developed by a team of 5, 3 programmers and 2 artists, in Unity.

I was responsible for and implemented:

  • Sound (creating and implementing)
  • In-Game UI (HUD, Pause Menu)
  • Player (movement, punching, spawning)
  • PickUp system (architecture, interactions, spawning) (but not the individual PickUp logic)
  • Input (custom, XInput backend -> more responsive, fixes Unity not supporting multiple XBOX360 controllers)

And also implemented a couple of tools while developing:

  • ParticleManager
  • SoundManager
  • UINavigator (support for navigating UI with multiple controllers)
  • Debug Prefab (sandbox of core game with script at the top level containing tweakable parameters)
  • PerforceDiscordWebhook

The game was also selected for the first edition of Game Pitch Café in June 2019, an event where the 10 best games of the year at Digital Arts & Entertainment present their game.
